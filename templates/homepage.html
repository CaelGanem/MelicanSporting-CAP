Melican Sporting Equipment

Exercise is an important part of having a healthy body and mind. Playing sports is a great way to get exercise. To get proper exercise by playing sports a person needs a place to play sports and proper equipment to use. Northborough has many athletic fields and courts but they are not being used as much as they could be. Making the athletic fields more easily available and providing proper equipment to borrow would help people exercise more.


The Facts:


  1. Being physically active while playing sports has many physical health benefits. “Being physically active can improve your brain health, help manage weight, reduce the risk of disease, strengthen bones and muscles, and improve your ability to do everyday activities” (“Benefits of Physical Activity”).

  2. The CDC says that 110,00 deaths per year could be prevented if US adults ages 40 and above increased their physical activity by even a small amount each day.

  3. Heart disease and stroke are two of the leading causes of death in the United States, and getting at least 150 minutes a week of moderate physical activity can help reduce your chances of getting these diseases.

With many physical benefits to being physically active, Northborough should be taking steps to get kids more active and involved with outdoor activities. This information also tells us that everybody should get into the habit of being physically active. Being physically active helps increase physical health and decreases deaths.

Emotional Health Benefits


  1. “Research shows the benefits of exercise go beyond just physical well-being. Physical activities - like walking, biking, dancing, yoga, or tai chi - can help: Reduce feelings of depression and stress, enhance your mood and overall emotional well-being, improve sleep, and increase your energy levels” (“The Emotional Benefits of Exercise”).

  2. Exercise has many benefits. It can help you feel happier, give you more energy and promote quality sleep. Exercise can also fight depression, maintain strong muscles and bones, reduce the risk of chronic disease, boost your brain health and make you more productive.

  3. These changes can help a person maintain weight and live longer (“13 Benefits of Exercise”).

Little steps such as exercising more frequently or adding ten minutes to your regular exercise routine can help improve your mental and emotional health. With so many benefits, there is no real downside to putting a little time into your day to be physically active.



The need to proper equipment


  1. Heartland College Sports states: “Playing sports without proper gear can be very dangerous, and, if you are not careful enough, a fun game can lead to a hospital visit. Wearing the proper gear reduces the chances of somebody getting injured, enabling you to focus on the game.”

  2. Proper equipment should always be applied when playing a sport. Playing a game for fun can have the same risks of injury as playing a game competitively (CP).

  3. Whether a person is playing a game with just their friends or on a sports team, they should always take precautions. Even if a person thinks they are safe, they should always be careful and considerate of the dangers of sports.

These guidelines can help us understand the precautions and safety measures needed to be safe in sports. Something could go wrong at any point in time, putting you or others in danger. If you are prepared and wearing proper equipment, you are much safer when participating in sporting activities.


There are some rules about using these areas though


Northborough already has a lot of sports fields and courts. These are great resources that could be used for exercise and playing sports but they are being underutilized because rules restrict when they are available. For example, at Melican Middle School, there are trespassing rules that prevent people from using courts and fields. Fields around Melican are blocked from use past 8 pm, making it difficult to use them. Most kids only get home from school around 3 pm, and many kids participate in after-school activities that take up their free time even more. A group of kids that would like to get exercise by playing sports in these fields would need to stay past 8 pm. The rules that make Melican fields inaccessible after 8 pm mean that they are rarely available or usable by the public.

In addition, even when public recreation areas in and around Northborough are available, people who want to play a sport in the areas may not have the proper equipment. There is also no public equipment available for the courts or fields around Melican. This means kids would have to bring their own equipment, which can be expensive, bulky, and hard to transport. It would be unlikely that a group of people would have brought equipment for each player to play a non-competitive game. Even if they planned to, not everybody has the correct or appropriate equipment for every sport. Proper equipment and storage are needed for people to be safe when participating in sports. “At any sports facility, there is potential for injuries due to the physical nature of sports and exercise. To reduce injury, it is important to make sure that all equipment is stored safely and securely. Additionally, the proper equipment must be used for each activity or sport” ( Important Safety Measures to Consider”). If the right equipment is used and stored correctly, a player should be safe while playing sports. Using the wrong equipment can be dangerous and cause injury when participating in sports. Equipment storage should be secure and safe for people to use (jumpsix2). If sports equipment were available to borrow, more people would be able to play sports as exercise.


Some Solutions:

  1. If access times were extended until later in the day and sports equipment were available in Northborough recreation areas, more people would use the fields. There are many physical and mental health benefits to being physically active through sports. If proper equipment and storage for the equipment were provided so that students could use the fields, it would help make students in Northborough more physically and mentally strong.

  2. If students were allowed to use the areas later than the current trespassing rules, it could make students more willing to use the spaces. If equipment loss or theft is happening, it could be solved by a system set up to control who can access the equipment.

  3. Northborough could provide a “vending machine” allowing students to scan their school ID cards or enter their school code to receive equipment that they would return once they had finished using it. A system like this could keep track of who takes out equipment and when they return it to help prevent theft and help keep the equipment safe. If the equipment was made accessible to people, or students were allowed on the fields/courts later than 8 pm, the number of people using the areas would increase.
